Dr. S. Swayamjyoti

Researcher in Computational Materials Science and Machine Learning

About

Dr. S. Swayamjyoti is a researcher specializing in computational materials science, machine learning, and computational mechanics. He earned his Doctorate of Science from ETH Zurich, focusing on atomistic simulations to study metallic glasses. Currently, he is affiliated with RWTH Aachen University in Germany.
